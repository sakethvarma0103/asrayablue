Asraya आश्रय

  • User Name

Asraya in Sanskrit means assistance. Asraya has been designed with an aim to bring all the Career Services being offered to our student community under one roof. It is a one stop solution from GCGG for the University students to avail services related to Counseling & Mentoring. Competence & Skill Development, and Career Fulfillment including Other Carer Options. Hence the platform is riled as Asraya.


GCGC Asraya is designed and developed to be part of every GITAMite's journey. This platform helps the students to interact with Mentors to seek guidance and support, participate in Competence and Skill Development activities conducted by Directorate of Competence Development and utilize the services of Career Fulfillment for Jobs and Internships along with Higher Education and Other Career Services
